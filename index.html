Demo Overview

This interactive demonstration showcases our research on memorization in lyrics-to-music generative models. We present evidence that state-of-the-art models like YuE can reproduce copyrighted songs with high fidelity when given original or phonetically-modified lyrics.

Key Findings: Through systematic testing with phoneme modifications (like "jingle bells" ‚Üí "jingle shells"), we demonstrate that AI music models exhibit significant memorization, raising important questions about copyright safety in generative music systems.
